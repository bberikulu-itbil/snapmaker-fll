OASIS — Optimal Artifact Storage Intelligence System

Automates storage: monitors environment, detects risk, captures evidence with AI camera, acts instantly.

ESP32 · sensors Raspberry Pi · AI Camera · evidence

Sensors

Full environment watch

  • Temp/humidity (DHT22/BME280)
  • Light (GY-30), air quality (CCS811), gas (MQ-4)
  • If out of range — fan, mist, LED, buzzer trigger

AI + data

Raspberry Pi analytics

  • Photo/video: detects color change, dust, condensate
  • Logs: incidents stored with timestamps
  • Auto-action: alarm, actuators, documentation

Thresholds

Normal vs Risk

  • Temp 18–22°C (risk >28°C)
  • Humidity 45–55% (risk >70%)
  • Light <50 lux (risk >300 lux)
  • CO₂ <1000 ppm (risk >1500 ppm)
