Innovation project

OASIS — citizen water-quality monitoring

Portable sensor + 3D-printed body + open data dashboard. Goal: make environmental measurements accessible to schools and clubs.

Temp · Turbidity · pH UART · BLE 3D printed body

Mechanics

Modular enclosure

  • Sensor module swaps via magnetic mount.
  • PETG, water-resistant sealing.
  • LEGO axle adapter for robot mounting.
Download GLB Ask the team →

Data flow

Short pipeline

  • UART → BLE: sensor packets go to BLE module.
  • MQTT: mobile app pushes to broker, Grafana dashboard.
  • Cache: offline logs to SD, resends when online.

Judging story

2-minute pitch

  • Problem: lack of accessible water-quality labs at schools.
  • Solution: OASIS device + open data panel.
  • Impact: students collect data, form hypotheses, take action.
